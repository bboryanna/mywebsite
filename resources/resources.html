CSS The position Property and CSS display Property In my opinion these two properties are the two most important to know when creating your website. Whenever an element was not showing up on the page the way i wantet in to it was most likely due to wrong configuration of one of these two properties. So yes If things are not showing up the way you would expect them check out if the problem is because of them. So many times I would be wondering, trying diffrent things and in the end teh solution will be most likely changing the display from inline to block for example. Familiarise yourself with them before starting anything and work your way up from the more simple stuff to the more complicated things. Know the basics.
